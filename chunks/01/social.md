## LinkedIn

> Building an AI agent on a free LLM is either a stunt or a discipline. I'm
> betting it's a discipline.
>
> Starting today: 40 days of Margeen, in public.
>
> You give the model one sentence — "find me blue kids pants with >15%
> margin" — and it has to do the rest. Search AliExpress. Cost the SKU out
> honestly. Ask you to approve. List on a dev Shopify store. Drop-ship.
> Watch the numbers.
>
> Drop-shipping isn't new. Agentic LLMs aren't new. What's new is the
> constraint: Gemini Flash's free tier, free SaaS, no paid models. If a
> free agent can't clear a 15% margin on a real AliExpress product, that's
> a finding — not a workaround.
>
> One article, one commit, one short post per day. For 40 days. If it
> fails, it fails on camera.
